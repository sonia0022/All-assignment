Specialized Markets

Enjoy The Unique Atmosphere Of Shopping By Purchasing Your Needs Of Various Goods And Products Offered By Dubai Specialized Markets, Which Have Been Prepared To Allow All Categories And Segments To Benefit From Them According To The Field Of Each Market. The Visitor And Resident In Dubai Can Obtain All The Needs And Requirements Of Various Commodities In One Designated Place, Taking Into Account The Provision Of All Services And Facilities In Each Market. Among These Markets Are Dubai Birds And Pets Market, Auto Market, Truck & Heavy Equipment Market, Falcon Heritage And Sports Centre, Al Fahidi Souk, Hatta Market, Deira Fish Market, Dubai Central Fruit & Vegetable Market, Al-Lisaili Camel Market, Naif Souq, Al Rashidiya Central Market, Hamriyah Central Market And Al Qusais Cattle Market.
